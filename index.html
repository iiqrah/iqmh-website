About Us

At Iqrah Montessori House, we’re proud to provide a caring and safe environment for your children. By following our mission, vision and values, we create a nurturing, stimulating and welcoming learning environment for all our children.

We provide our students with the tools they need for future happiness and success. Our unique programs incorporate a variety of educational theories, while always keeping the child’s best interests in mind and allowing for hands-on, experiential learning. Get in touch to learn more!

Admission

Admission for the Year 2022-2023 is now open.

In order to enroll your child, please call us (089811 28307) to schedule your visit to the school.

The School Office operates Monday to Friday from 8:30 am to 12:00 noon.

You have to submit the following in the time of admission:

  • Filled-in and signed Admission Form (to be obtained from School Office upon payment of Admission Fee)
  • Copy of the child's Birth Certificate
  • Copy of the child's child's Aadhar Card
  • Recent coloured passport size photograph of your child (2 copies)
  • First month's fee

Please contact the School Office for the current fee structure and any other queries you might have.
